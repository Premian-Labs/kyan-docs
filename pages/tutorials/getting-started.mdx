# Getting Started

Kyan is a decentralized derivatives exchange for crypto options and perpetual futures. All trading happens on-chain, so you will need a Web3 wallet to get started.

<Callout type="info">
  **Prerequisites**

  - A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, or any compatible provider)
  - ETH for gas fees
  - USDC for trading collateral
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Connect Your Wallet

Navigate to [app.kyan.blue](https://app.kyan.blue) and click **Connect Wallet** in the top-right corner. Select your wallet provider from the list and approve the connection request in your wallet's popup. Once connected, your truncated address will appear in the header.

### Approve USDC

Before you can deposit, you need to grant the Kyan smart contract permission to access your USDC. Click **Approve USDC** when prompted and confirm the approval transaction in your wallet. This is a one-time step per token.

### Deposit Collateral

Navigate to the **Deposit** page. Enter the amount of USDC you want to deposit and click **Deposit**. Confirm the transaction in your wallet. Your balance will update once the transaction is confirmed on-chain, which typically takes a few seconds.

### Explore the Interface

Take a moment to familiarize yourself with the main trading dashboard:

- **Order book** -- live bids and asks for the selected market
- **Chart** -- price history with configurable timeframes and indicators
- **Position panel** -- your open positions, active orders, and trade history
- **Margin summary** -- available balance, margin usage, and account health

</Steps>
