# Your First Combo Trade

<Callout type="tip">
  New to combos? Read [Combo Orders](/concepts/combo-orders) to understand multi-leg execution.
</Callout>

Combo trades let you execute multiple option legs as a single atomic order -- all legs fill together or none of them do. This is how you build strategies like vertical spreads, straddles, and iron condors on Kyan.

<Callout type="info">
  **Prerequisites**

  - Completed at least one options trade -- see [Your First Options Trade](/tutorials/first-options-trade)
  - Familiar with options terminology (strikes, expiries, calls, puts)
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Open the Combo Builder

Click **Combo** in the order panel to open the multi-leg builder. The builder provides a workspace where you can assemble multiple option legs into a single package.

### Add the First Leg

Click **Add Leg** and configure the first option. For example, to build a bull call spread, start by buying a call at a lower strike:

- **Expiry** -- select your target expiration date
- **Strike** -- choose a strike price near or slightly below the current market price
- **Type** -- Call
- **Side** -- Buy
- **Quantity** -- number of contracts

### Add the Second Leg

Click **Add Leg** again and configure the opposing leg. For the bull call spread, sell a call at a higher strike with the same expiry:

- **Strike** -- choose a strike price above the first leg
- **Type** -- Call
- **Side** -- Sell
- **Quantity** -- same as the first leg

This creates a defined-risk spread where your max loss is the net premium paid and your max profit is the difference between strikes minus the premium.

### Review the Strategy

The builder displays a combined view of your strategy:

- **Net premium** -- total cost (debit) or credit received
- **Max profit** -- the best-case outcome at expiry
- **Max loss** -- the worst-case outcome at expiry
- **Breakeven points** -- the price levels where the strategy transitions between profit and loss

### Submit the Combo Order

Click **Submit Combo Order**. The order executes atomically -- every leg fills or the entire order is cancelled. Confirm the transaction in your wallet and watch the fills appear in the order log.

### Monitor the Strategy

Your combo position appears in the **Positions** panel as a grouped entry. Track the combined P&L across all legs, and review how the position behaves as the underlying price moves and time passes.

</Steps>
